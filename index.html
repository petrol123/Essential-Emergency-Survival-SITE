How to Use This Guide

This guide is organized by survival priority. In an emergency, start at Section 1 and work forward. For specific reference information, go directly to the relevant section. Tables and checklists are designed for quick scanning under stress.

Keep a printed copy in your home, vehicle, and bug‑out bag.

Section 1 — Immediate Response (Preview)

The 3‑Step Survival Mindset

1. ACCEPT

Acknowledge that a real emergency is happening. Denial costs critical minutes.

2. PLAN

Assess your situation before moving. Identify threats and resources.

3. ACT

Execute decisively. A good plan executed now beats a perfect plan executed too late.
