Section 12 — Quick Reference

Fast Survival Facts, Ratios, and Emergency Actions

Critical Survival Timelines

  • 3 minutes — without air
  • 3 hours — without shelter (in harsh weather)
  • 3 days — without water
  • 3 weeks — without food

Water Purification

  • Boil 1 minute (3 minutes at altitude)
  • Bleach: 8 drops per gallon
  • Iodine tablets: follow package instructions
  • Solar disinfection: 6 hours in full sun

Fire Starting Essentials

  • Three elements: heat, fuel, oxygen
  • Best tinder: birch bark, cotton balls, dryer lint
  • Best ignition: lighter → ferro rod → matches
  • Fire lays: teepee, log cabin, lean‑to, Dakota hole

First Aid Priorities

  • Stop bleeding
  • Open airway
  • Treat shock
  • Prevent infection
  • Immobilize injuries

Navigation Basics

  • Sun rises east, sets west
  • North Star = true north
  • Shadow stick method: first mark = west
  • Three whistle blasts = distress

Food Safety

  • Cook meat thoroughly
  • Never eat unknown mushrooms
  • When in doubt, throw it out
  • Store food away from sleeping area

Power & Electronics

  • Use low‑brightness modes
  • Airplane mode saves battery
  • Charge power banks first, then devices
  • Keep batteries warm in cold weather

Sanitation Rules

  • Wash hands before eating
  • Dispose of waste 200 ft from water
  • Boil or purify all drinking water
  • Keep wounds clean and covered

Emergency Signals

  • Three fires in a triangle = SOS
  • Mirror flashes visible for miles
  • Bright colors increase visibility
  • Smoke signals: green vegetation for white smoke
